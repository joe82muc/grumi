Going-to Future

Unit 4 Grammar - mit KI-Assistent pro Übung

Infotafel: Regeln, Aussage, Verneinung, Frage

AussageI am / You are / He is + going to + Verb (base form).
Example: We are going to apply for jobs.
Verneinungam not / is not / are not + going to + Verb.
Example: She is not going to leave today.
FrageAm / Is / Are + subject + going to + Verb?
Example: Are you going to visit Wellington?
KurzregelNutze going to fuer feste Plaene und Vorhersagen mit Hinweis (Look! ...).
